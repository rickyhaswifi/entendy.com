La Luna Restaurant

is a restaurant that serves a variety of delicious Mexican cuisine, from tacos to chile relleno. In addition to great Mexican dishes, we treat each customer like they are family, so when you are looking for a great place to eat, try our southwestern-style restaurant for the best food and service in town. Our partner-owned restaurant opened in June 2003, but we have more than 8 years of experience in the restaurant business.
